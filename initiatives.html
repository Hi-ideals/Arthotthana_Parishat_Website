Initiatives Driving Change

Heritage & Tourism

Namma Bidar

A dedicated community platform celebrating the rich history, culture, and tourism of Bidar, Karnataka. Namma Bidar aims to boost local economies by connecting travelers and local guides, promoting historical landmarks, and showcasing the heritage of the region.

Local Commerce

Localight

An innovative technology platform empowering neighborhood micro-businesses, farmers, and skilled workers. Localight digitizes the local economy, enabling small merchants to easily connect with regional consumers and compete in the digital era.

Culture & Arts

Parv (Coming Soon)

A celebration of culture, art, and local talent. Parv is an upcoming initiative designed to organize cultural festivals, spotlight regional artists, and create platforms for youth to engage with traditional art forms while generating sustainable creative opportunities.
