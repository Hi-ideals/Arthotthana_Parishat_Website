Who We Are

Making Karnataka's Youth Unstoppable

Arthotthana Parishat is a registered society based in Bidar, Karnataka, dedicated to the economic upliftment of unemployed youth, women entrepreneurs, and marginalised communities across the State of Karnataka. We believe every young person deserves a chance to become a job creator, not just a job seeker.

  • Workshops for self-employed professionals, businessmen & startups
  • Digital platforms for farmers, village entrepreneurs & small businesses
  • Promoting "Vocal for Local" — supporting local products over imports
  • Human Resources Development & Educational Institutions support
  • Medical facilities & Rural Health Clinics for rural communities
