Our Story & Mission

Driven by purpose. Guided by community. Measured by impact.

Who We Are

Arthotthana Parishat

Arthotthana Parishat is a registered society headquartered at H.No. 9-8-54, Gurupad Nilaya, BVB College Road, Vidyanagar, Bidar – 585401, operating under the laws of the State of Karnataka.

Founded with a singular purpose — to work for the economic development and welfare of unemployed youth — we have grown into a multi-dimensional organisation touching education, healthcare, digital empowerment, and entrepreneurship across Karnataka.

We are not just an NGO. We are a movement of ordinary people doing extraordinary things for the communities they call home.

Our Direction

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

A Karnataka where every young person — regardless of caste, creed, or social status — has the tools, knowledge, and opportunity to become an entrepreneur, a job creator, and a proud contributor to their community and country.

Our Mission

To empower unemployed youth, women entrepreneurs, farmers, and rural communities through targeted workshops, digital platforms, educational support, healthcare access, and mentorship programs — turning aspirations into sustainable enterprises.

Founder's Message

A Note from Our President

"When we founded Arthotthana Parishat, we had one simple belief: that unemployment is not a personal failure — it is a systemic gap that society must bridge together. Our country has millions of talented, passionate young people who only need the right door opened for them. We are that door."

Every program we run, every workshop we conduct, every health camp we organise — it all flows from this conviction. We have seen farmers double their incomes using digital tools we taught them. We have watched women who never ran a business become proud employers in their villages.

The journey has not been easy, but the impact keeps us going. I invite you to join our movement — as a volunteer, a donor, or simply as someone who believes in the power of community.

Our Journey

Organisation Timeline

2014
Foundation
Arthotthana Parishat registered as a society in Karnataka with a focus on economic development of unemployed youth in Bidar.
2016
First Entrepreneur Workshop
Conducted our first large-scale startup workshop for 200 youth in Bidar District, producing 45 new micro-enterprises.
2018
Women Empowerment Mela
Launched annual Women Entrepreneurs' Mela, bringing together 300+ women from across Karnataka to showcase local products.
2020
Digital Platform Launch
Deployed e-commerce platforms for farmers and village entrepreneurs during COVID-19, connecting them to online markets.
2022
Rural Health Initiative
Launched Mobile Health Unit program, conducting free medical camps in 40+ villages across Bidar and Yadgir districts.
2024
10 Years of Impact
Celebrating a decade of service — 5,000+ youth impacted, 800+ entrepreneurs created, and programs across 12 Karnataka districts.
What Guides Us

Our Core Values

Integrity
Every rupee donated is accounted for. We operate with full transparency and accountability to our communities and supporters.
Inclusivity
We serve all communities irrespective of caste, creed, or social status. No one is left behind in our programs.
Innovation
We embrace digital tools and creative approaches to solve old problems — from e-commerce for farmers to app-based skilled worker platforms.
Compassion
Every person we serve is treated with dignity and respect. We exist because we genuinely care about the communities we work in.
