Volunteer With Us

Your skills and time can transform lives — join our growing community of changemakers.

Why Give Your Time

Benefits of Volunteering

Certificate of Appreciation
Receive an official recognition letter and certificate from Arthotthana Parishat for your service and contributions.
Expand Your Network
Connect with entrepreneurs, educators, healthcare workers, and development professionals across Karnataka.
Skill Development
Gain hands-on experience in program management, community outreach, digital training, and social impact work.
Real Impact
See the tangible difference your efforts make — from youth starting businesses to families accessing healthcare for the first time.
Open Roles

Volunteer Opportunities

We welcome volunteers from all backgrounds. Whether you have 2 hours a week or can commit to a full program — there's a role for you.

Digital Trainer
4–6 hrs/week  |  Remote or On-site

Teach e-commerce, social media, and digital marketing skills to entrepreneurs and farmers.

Healthcare Volunteer
Event-based  |  Rural Karnataka

Doctors, nurses and paramedics needed for mobile health camps in rural areas.

Workshop Facilitator
Weekends  |  Bidar & Districts

Facilitate startup workshops, leadership sessions, and skill-building events for youth.

Content & Media
Flexible  |  Remote

Photographers, videographers, writers and social media managers to document our work.
