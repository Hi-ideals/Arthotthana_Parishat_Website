Objectives of Arthotthana Parishat

  1. To work for the economic development and welfare of unemployed youth across Karnataka.
  2. To organize entrepreneurship development programs, business workshops, seminars, conferences, training sessions, and awareness programs for youth, self-employed professionals, startups, and entrepreneurs.
  3. To establish digital platforms, marketing networks, and e-commerce solutions for farmers, village entrepreneurs, artisans, skilled workers, and local businesses.
  4. To promote the concept of “Vocal for Local” and encourage the production, promotion, marketing, and consumption of indigenous and locally manufactured products.
  5. To organize exhibitions, melas, trade fairs, entrepreneurship development camps, and awareness programs for women entrepreneurs, self-help groups, and rural communities.
  6. To establish, support, manage, and promote educational institutions from pre-primary level to higher education, including teacher training institutes, vocational centers, and skill development academies.
  7. To establish and maintain libraries, hostels, coaching centers, training facilities, research centers, and educational support services for students and youth.
  8. To undertake Human Resource Development programs for students, professionals, entrepreneurs, and unemployed youth.
  9. To establish, operate, and support Rural Health Clinics, Mobile Health Units, Hospitals, Diagnostic Centers, and community healthcare facilities.
  10. To provide medical assistance, healthcare services, and welfare support for economically weaker sections, children, women, senior citizens, and rural communities.
  11. To create digital and physical platforms connecting skilled workers such as carpenters, electricians, plumbers, mechanics, engineers, doctors, accountants, and other professionals with employment and business opportunities.
  12. To collaborate with Government Departments, Educational Institutions, Corporate Organizations, Financial Institutions, NGOs, and International Agencies for community development and social welfare initiatives.
  13. To undertake activities related to environmental protection, sanitation, water conservation, renewable energy, sustainable development, and ecological awareness.
  14. To conduct awareness campaigns and educational programs related to health, education, entrepreneurship, employment generation, financial literacy, digital literacy, and social welfare.
  15. To receive grants, donations, subscriptions, sponsorships, contributions, endowments, and other lawful sources of funding for achieving the objectives of the organization.
  16. To acquire, lease, maintain, develop, transfer, or dispose of movable and immovable properties necessary for carrying out the activities and objectives of the organization.
  17. To undertake any lawful activity that promotes social welfare, economic development, entrepreneurship, community empowerment, rural development, and sustainable growth.
  18. To collaborate with Government Departments, Educational Institutions, Corporate Organizations, Financial Institutions, NGOs, Trusts, Societies, Community-Based Organizations, and International Agencies for the implementation of social welfare, economic development, educational, healthcare, entrepreneurship, and community development initiatives.
  19. To support, facilitate, organize, coordinate, and manage conferences, workshops, seminars, exhibitions, awareness campaigns, training programs, cultural programs, healthcare camps, entrepreneurship events, and other social welfare activities independently or in collaboration with partner organizations.
  20. To enter into partnerships, collaborations, Memorandums of Understanding (MoUs), and joint arrangements with organizations, institutions, associations, trusts, societies, and agencies for conducting joint events, programs, projects, and community development initiatives.
  21. To support any organization in conducting events and collaborative programs and, on behalf of such events, collect registrations, sponsorships, donations, grants, contributions, and other funds and remit or utilize the same strictly in accordance with the terms of the agreement entered into between the parties and applicable laws.
  22. To act as an implementation partner, event management partner, project coordinator, resource organization, knowledge partner, or facilitation agency for programs undertaken by Government bodies, private institutions, NGOs, and social organizations.

Membership & Participation

  • Membership may be granted to individuals who support the aims and objectives of Arthotthana Parishat.
  • Members shall comply with the rules, regulations, and policies adopted by the organization from time to time.
  • The organization reserves the right to suspend or terminate membership for misconduct, unlawful activities, or actions contrary to its objectives.

Donations & Contributions

  • All donations and contributions received by the organization shall be utilized for charitable, educational, healthcare, entrepreneurship, and community development activities.
  • Donors shall not claim ownership, management rights, or control over the affairs of the organization.
  • Donations once made shall generally be non-refundable except where required by applicable law.

Volunteer Participation

  • Volunteers shall act ethically, responsibly, and professionally while representing the organization.
  • Volunteers shall respect beneficiaries, staff members, community members, and fellow volunteers.
  • The organization reserves the right to discontinue volunteer participation in cases of misconduct or violation of organizational policies.
