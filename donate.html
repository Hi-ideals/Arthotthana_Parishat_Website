Common Questions

Donation FAQ

Is my donation eligible for tax exemption?
Yes. Arthotthana Parishat is registered under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act. Donations made to us are eligible for tax deductions. You will receive an official receipt with all details needed for your tax filing.
How will my donation be used?
100% of your donation goes directly to our programs — entrepreneurship workshops, digital skill training, women empowerment events, healthcare camps, and educational support. We publish annual impact reports with detailed financial disclosures.
Can I donate in someone else's name / as a memorial gift?
Absolutely. Use the "Message" field during donation to specify the name of the person you are donating in honour or memory of. We can issue a certificate of dedication on request.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), online bank transfer (NEFT/RTGS/IMPS), credit/debit cards, and cheques or demand drafts payable to "Arthotthana Parishat".
Can I set up a recurring monthly donation?
Yes! Recurring donations are a powerful way to support us. Please contact us at info@arthotthanaparishat.org or call us to set up a monthly giving arrangement and we'll walk you through the simple process.
Will I receive confirmation of my donation?
Yes. You will receive an email confirmation within 24 hours of your donation, along with an official receipt. For donations above ₹500, we also send a personalised thank-you letter from our President.
